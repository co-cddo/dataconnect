DataConnect23

Welcome to DataConnect23

This year's data conference series is taking place from Monday 25 September to Friday 29 September 2023 and our programme is now live.

The programme features over 100 sessions, led by a wide range of government departments, universities, institutes and other public sector organisations.

We invite you to register for individual, virtual sessions now, and you will receive joining information prior to the event in September.

We hope you network, innovate and upskill at this year's event.

If you have any specific accessibility requirements, please contact us.

Book DataConnect23 sessions

This week-long virtual event celebrates the power of data, and is facilitated by the Central Digital and Data Office (CDDO) and Data Quality Hub (DQHub), part of the Office for National Statistics (ONS).

DataConnect brings together data experts from across central government, local government, devolved administrations, academia and national institutes. It has a diverse agenda created for, and led by, the public sector data community.
