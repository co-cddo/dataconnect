DataConnect23

Monday 25 September 2023

DataConnect23 is a week-long series of presentations, webinars, show and tells, demonstrations, case studies, discussions and panels. The programme is open to all and is delivered by the community, for the community.

The Central Digital and Data Office in Cabinet Office and the Data Quality Hub in ONS coordinate DataConnect23 on behalf of participants.

Bring it together

Any and all data topics are welcome at DataConnect23. We want you to bring your data issues, projects and contexts, technical and non technical - from service delivery, to data science, to interoperability, to algorithms, to access, to linking, to APIs, to standards, to analytics, to ethics, to tooling, to management, to architecture, and beyond.

If you work in data in the UK public sector and would like to host and run a session in September, then we welcome you to take part. We are accepting session ideas until Friday 14th July.

10am to 11am on 25 September 2023

Leveraging data science could be the most effective way for policymakers to make evidence-based decisions. Given changes in policy are often associated with interconnected systems such as urban systems, climate change, healthcare provision and social infrastructure, we need a way to enable policymakers to connect data across these sectors.

We introduce the Digital Solutions Hub and discuss how integrating environmental; socioeconomic and health data can address policy challenges from national and local scale.
