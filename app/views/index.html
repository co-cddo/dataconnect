DataConnect23

Welcome to DataConnect23

This year's data conference series is taking place from Monday 25 September to Friday 29 September 2023 and our programme is now live.

The programme features over 100 sessions, led by a wide range of government departments, universities, institutes and other public sector organisations.

We invite you to register for individual, virtual sessions now, and you will receive joining information prior to the event in September.

The conference will be launched at 9am on Monday 25 September, by Minister Alex Burghart, Parliamentary Secretary for the Cabinet Office, and followed by a high profile panel discussion with Megan Lee Devlin, CEO, CDDO, Christophe Prince, Director Data and Identity at UK Home Office, and John Kelly, Chief Data Officer - HM Treasury.

Be sure to book places at these two launch sessions, and listen to the minister’s overview and vision for the conference, and the panel’s insight on data leadership.

We hope you network, innovate and upskill at this year's event.

If you have any specific accessibility requirements, please contact us.

Book DataConnect23 sessions

This week-long virtual event celebrates the power of data, and is facilitated by the Central Digital and Data Office (CDDO) and Data Quality Hub (DQHub), part of the Office for National Statistics (ONS).

DataConnect brings together data experts from across central government, local government, devolved administrations, academia and national institutes. It has a diverse agenda created for, and led by, the public sector data community.

Sessions spotlight

Building a principled data culture in UKHSA

Find out how the UK Health Security Agency is building a data culture around five data principles: Responsible, Accessible, Trustworthy, Efficient, and Innovative.

Delivering system change via linked data

Learn about a project that supports individuals with complex needs, with ‘Better Outcomes through Linked Data', a data linking initiative involving MoJ, DHSC, DLUHC and the Welsh Government.

Simplifying offences across the Criminal Justice System

Hear how the Ministry of Justice shares, publishes, and makes available offence data to any team that needs it via modern APIs.

DataConnect launch with Minister Alex Burghart

Join Minister Alex Burghart, Parliamentary Secretary for the Cabinet Office, to hear his opening speech welcoming everyone to DataConnect.

Virtual launch panel discussion - Data leadership and vision

Sue Bateman, Interim Chief Data Officer at CDDO will host a high level panel discussion about offering a vision of data and inspiring leadership across government.

Unleashing the Power of Data: Transforming Government Decision-Making

Alison Pritchard, Deputy National Statistician at ONS will discuss the immense potential of data in empowering government with valuable insights and driving innovation.

A diverse agenda

This year’s programme includes over 100 events, delivered by a range of government departments including the Ministry of Defence (MoD), 10 Data Science (10DS), Ministry of Justice (MoJ), Home Office and Ordnance Survey, as well as Welsh Government, The University of Manchester, Devon County Council and The Alan Turing Institute.

Sessions cover a range of different topics, including ChatGPT and large language models, data ethics, using data to support levelling up, future farming schemes, workforce planning and interoperability across government.
