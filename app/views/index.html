25 - 29 September 2023

DataConnect is a week-long series of presentations, webinars, show and tells, demonstrations, case studies, discussions and panels. The programme is open, user-generated and mostly delivered online. The Central Digital and Data Office at Cabinet Office and the Data Quality Hub at ONS coordinate DataConnect on behalf of participants.

Any and all data topics are welcome at DataConnect23. We want you to bring your data issues, projects and contexts, technical and non technical - from service delivery, to data science, to interoperability, to algorithms, to access, to linking, to APIs, to standards, to analytics, to ethics, to tooling, to management, to architecture, and beyond.

Bring it together

To continue to deliver with data and realise our data ambitions, we need to #BringItTogether. ‘Together’ is when we work as multidisciplinary teams, or bridge different systems, maximise interoperability, enable service delivery across organisations or when sharing our data, our skills and our insights. DataConnect is a special opportunity to make those connections between people and programmes; because our work is strengthened and our goals easier to reach when we enable different data initiatives, skills, services, technologies and, most importantly, people, to come together.
