DataConnect23 is coming

DataConnect is a week-long ‘everything data’ series of presentations, webinars, show and tells, demonstrations, case studies, discussions and panels. The programme is open, user-generated and mostly delivered online. The Central Digital and Data Office at Cabinet Office and the Data Quality Hub at ONS coordinate DataConnect on behalf of participants.

Any and all data topics are welcome at DataConnect23. We want you to bring your data issues, projects and contexts, technical and non technical - from service delivery, to data science, to interoperability, to algorithms, to access, to linking, to APIs, to standards, to analytics, to ethics, to tooling, to management, to architecture, and beyond.

Bring it together

DataConnect is about achieving a better view of the breadth and depth of data work happening across the public sector and establishing stronger connections between people and programmes. Our strapline for 2023 - Bring it together - reflects the DataConnect aim to bring together various combinations of people, skills, datasets, ideas, systems, services, learnings, standards, organisations and users to achieve our shared and individual data goals.

DataConnect is by and for you

DataConnect is delivered by the UK public sector for the UK public sector. We welcome presenters and attendees who are at the start of their career to experienced leaders, from central government, devolved administrations, arm’s length bodies, local government, health, policing and security and other public bodies and services. You might have a defined DDaT or KIM role, or be coming from policy, operational delivery, legal, finance, HR, communications, commercial, counter fraud, or project management perspectives.

Previous DataConnect weeks have featured sessions from the Welsh Government, Ordnance Survey, Office for National Statistics, DWP Digital, Barnsley Metropolitan Borough Council, Sport England, GDS, Scottish Government, Department for Education, Local Government Association, Gambling Commission, National Highways, Maritime and Coastguard Agency, Home Office, and many more.
