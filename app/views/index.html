DataConnect23

25 - 29 September 2023

DataConnect23 is a week-long series of presentations, webinars, show and tells, demonstrations, case studies, discussions and panels. The programme is open to all and is delivered by the community, for the community.

The Central Digital and Data Office in Cabinet Office and the Data Quality Hub in ONS coordinate DataConnect23 on behalf of participants.

Bring it together

Any and all data topics are welcome at DataConnect23. We want you to bring your data issues, projects and contexts, technical and non technical - from service delivery, to data science, to interoperability, to algorithms, to access, to linking, to APIs, to standards, to analytics, to ethics, to tooling, to management, to architecture, and beyond.

Check back soon to see the schedule of events. We look forward to seeing you in September.
