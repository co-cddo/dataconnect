DataConnect23

Tuesday 26 September

The IDS Dissemination platform

9.15am: This session will present the Integrated Data Service dissemination platform: a data platform and APIs to support flexible access to stats.

Automating Metadata management to improve FAIR principles and data maturity

10am: This session will discuss automating the extraction of metadata with its curation, analysis, visualisation for various data users.

Driving Cultural Change by Treating Data as a Business Asset

10.30am: In this talk, attendees will discover how to recognise data as an asset, create organisation-wide buy-in, and change their data culture.

Establishing a Data Academy in the Maritime and Coastguard Agency

10.45am: In this session, the MCA will share how their Data Academy has empowered data citizens, data experts, and senior leadership teams.

Addressing the challenges with cross-government Reference and Master Data

11am: This session will share CDDO's collaborative work with departments to improve how reference and master data are managed and used.

The ONS Administrative data toolkit

11am: The session shares an administrative data toolkit that the Methodological Research Hub at ONS is developing and seeks your feedback.

So what? Communicating and getting value from your Data Maturity Assessment results

11am: Hear from a range of organisations who have recently completed a targeted data maturity assessment and how they are using their results.

An intelligent tool for identifying themes in text documents

12pm: This session will cover the inception of the Insights Engine tool by GIAA, why and how we developed it and what we plan to do with it in future.

UK Geospatial Strategy 2030: Unlocking the power of location

12pm: This session will outline the UK Geospatial Strategy 2030, positioning the UK to fully realise geospatial benefits across the economy.

The Civil Service Data Challenge

12pm: Launching Data Challenge for all civil servants, chaired by Vicki Chauhran, from Global Government Forum.

Asset data: standardised specification of information requirements

12pm: Learn about the 'Interoperability Code of Practice for Technologies in the Built and Managed Environment' to ensure that asset data is not 'locked' into software applications.

A Shared Services Centre of Excellence for Data

1pm: In this session, you'll learn about the Shared Services Centre of Excellence for Data (CoED), created to improve data management.

Rescuing orphan analogue environmental data at the Environment Agency

1pm: In this interactive workshop, share your experiences of digitising data and help us explore alternative methods of digitising data.

Unlocking VOA data: A journey through space and time

1.30pm: This session will cover the data transformation underway at Valuation Office Agency, and capability building that leads to better use of good quality data.

Geospatial data standards development

2.30pm: Ordnance Survey will explain the role of the international data standards community and Q-FAIR in geospatial data standards development.

Using a shared common language to support data literacy and understanding

3pm: This session will explain the ONS linked Business Glossary and its development stages.

Integrated Data Service: trailblazing data at your fingertips

3.30pm: This session will explain an exciting cross-government initiative, transforming how de-identified data is made available for vital research.

Building a principled data culture in UKHSA

4pm: Head of Data Strategy, Dr Matt Thompson discusses the launch of the UK Health Security Agency data strategy.

Panel discussion - AI in Public Services: Challenges and Opportunities

4pm: The panel will discuss the challenges and opportunities involved in bringing AI into public services, including a discussion of the latest advances in generative AI.
