DataConnect23

A week of sessions from 25 September to 29 September

DataConnect23 brings together teams from across the UK public sector
to share ideas, collaborate and learn from each other.

All of the sessions are virtual and are delivered by the community, for the community.

Register for sessions that you are interested in, and you will
receive joining information prior to the event in September.

If you have any specific accessibility requirements, please contact us.

Tuesday 26 September

9.15am to 10.00am

Office for National Statistics and TPXImpact

The IDS Dissemination platform: APIs and a platform for interoperable statistics

ONS and TPXimpact will present the Integrated Data Service dissemination platform: a data platform and APIs to support flexible access to a wide ranging collection of government stats.

10.00am to 11.00am

Department for Business & Trade

Automating an adaptive Metadata management to improve FAIR principles and data maturity

This session will discuss automating the extraction of metadata with its curation, analysis, visualisation for data users and consumers. Find out about FAIR principles and data maturity, and an adaptable “metadata” architecture.

10.45am to 11.30am

Maritime & Coastguard Agency

Establishing a Data Academy

The MCA Data Academy is designed to educate and empower data citizens, data experts, and senior leadership teams. Learn how the organisation developed a programme of apprenticeships, training, certification, communities and career pathways to utilise the power of data.
