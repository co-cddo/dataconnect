The IDS Dissemination platform: APIs and a platform for interoperable statistics - ONS

Tuesday 26 September, 9.15am to 10.00am

ONS and TPXimpact will present the Integrated Data Service dissemination platform: a data platform and APIs to support flexible access to a wide ranging collection of government stats. But having an API doesn't solve all the problems: the underlying data needs to be interoperable and standards-based too. We brought together data owners across government, defining standards and supporting them with tools and advice to create a high quality set of interoperable data.

Automating an adaptive Metadata management to improve FAIR principles and data maturity - Department for Business and Trade

Tuesday 26 September, 10.00am to 11.00am

Disparate data sources - and their metadata sources - can create an inconsistent and subjective understanding of data asset. A failure of identifying data redundancies can prevent consuming data to support to decision making.  Some enterprise metadata management may become costly and restrictive.  We, therefore, propose to discuss automating the extraction of metadata with its curation, analysis, visualisation for various data users and consumers. FAIR principles and data maturity will be demonstrated through a presentation, including an adaptable “metadata” architecture
